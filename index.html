❄️ Snowman Rescue ❄️

Guess the winter word before the snowman melts!

How to Play:

  • A secret winter-themed word is chosen automatically.
  • Click the buttons on the on-screen keyboard to guess letters.
  • Correct letters are revealed in the word display.
  • Incorrect guesses melt the snowman. You have 5 chances.
  • You may use one hint each round.
  • If you wish to stop early, click “Reveal Word.”
  • Click “Play Again” to start a new round.
